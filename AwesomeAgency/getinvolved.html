Work for Us


Our cooperative has a significant amount of expertise in accounting, tenant and property law, management, self-governance models, marketing, architecture, carpentry, and electrical work. We simply couldn't afford to make this project work if we didn't have this level of on-team expertise and passion. We dedicate countless hours, with the project as our only reward. It's rare that we need external help, and given all the expertise on our team, even rarer that we must hire someone to do this labor for us. But our expertise and our time have their limits. When we must reach out for help, we prefer to approach our community first. We're also aware that not everyone who wants to be involved can contribute fiscally, though they may have some spare cycles for pro-bono work.
